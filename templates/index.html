A free math competition led by BISV students to get students in the Silicon Valley to compete and highlight the wonders of mathematics. We are affiliated with Basis Independent Silicon Valley.

Solve Problems

Solve unique and intricate problems to improve your mathematical and problem solving ability as well as solidifying your foundations.

Learn More

Learn new ways to use your favorite mathematical thinking skills and formulas to solve innovative and complex problems created by our team!

Have Fun

Enjoy the problems! Fun and interesting problems are hard to come around, so enjoy the thrill of seeing these problems first-hand at our competition!

Mission

The BIMCT aims to provide fun and unique questions for participants. We hope to inspire more students to pursue mathematics, physics, and computer programming by demonstrating that they can be beautiful subjects utilizing creativity and intuition. We also aim to provide insight into how people combine these fields into other STEM fields; some problems may have implications in different areas of mathematics or STEM outside of the scope of the competition, but prior knowledge of those fields is not required.
